Our Story

Leading Limestone Production & Supply Company

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the limestone industry in Nigeria, Limestone Processing Links Limited has grown from a modest operation to a leading force in limestone production and processing. Our journey began with a single quarry and a commitment to quality that remains unchanged today.

Over the years, we've assembled a team of industry experts to ensure we deliver products that meet the highest standards. Our limestone is sourced from the richest deposits and processed with meticulous attention to detail.

Our Commitment

We are committed to sustainable mining practices, environmental responsibility, and the highest standards of product quality.

Our Approach

Combining traditional expertise with modern technology to deliver limestone products that exceed industry standards.

Our Journey

Company History & Milestones

2010

Company founded with a single quarry operation in Kano State, Nigeria, focusing on local limestone supply.

2013

Expanded operations with the acquisition of additional quarry sites and installation of first processing plant.

2016

Launched our fertilizer-grade limestone products, becoming a key supplier to Nigeria's agricultural sector.

2019

Implemented advanced processing technology, doubling production capacity while improving product quality.

2022

Expanded our customer base to include international clients across West Africa, establishing ourselves as a regional leader.

2024

Launched our sustainability initiative, committing to environmentally responsible mining and processing practices.

Company Facts

Key Facts About Our Operations

Discover what makes us a leader in the limestone processing industry through these key facts about our company and operations.

Our state-of-the-art processing facilities can handle over 50,000 metric tons of limestone monthly, ensuring we meet both large and small-scale customer demands efficiently.

We implement rigorous quality control measures at every stage of production, from quarrying to final processing, ensuring our limestone meets international quality standards.

We've invested in dust suppression systems, water recycling, and land reclamation programs to minimize our environmental impact and promote sustainable mining practices.

Our team comprises over 150 skilled professionals, including geologists, engineers, and quality control specialists, all dedicated to delivering exceptional limestone products.
