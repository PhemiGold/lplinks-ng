Agricultural Limestone

Specially processed to improve soil quality and enhance crop productivity

Product Description

Our agricultural limestone is carefully processed to provide the optimal particle size distribution for effective soil amendment. It neutralizes soil acidity, supplies essential calcium and magnesium, and improves soil structure for better root development and nutrient uptake.

Technical Specifications

Parameter Specification
Calcium Carbonate Equivalent ≥ 90%
Particle Size (Fine Grade) 100% passing through 2mm sieve
Particle Size (Coarse Grade) 90% passing through 5mm sieve
Moisture Content ≤ 2%
Magnesium Content 3-12% (depending on source)

Available Packaging Options

  • 50kg bags
  • 1-ton bulk bags
  • Bulk loose delivery

Applications

Crop Production

Improves soil pH for optimal crop growth, enhances nutrient availability, and provides essential calcium for plant cell wall development.

Pasture Improvement

Used in pasture lands to correct soil acidity, promoting healthier grass growth and improving forage quality for livestock.

Fertilizer Production

Serves as a key ingredient in fertilizer formulations, providing calcium and acting as a pH buffer in blended fertilizers.
